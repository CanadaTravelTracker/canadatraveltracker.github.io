## 1. Initial Setup & Onboarding

When you first open the app, follow these steps to ensure your calculations are accurate:

1.  **Welcome Screen**: Tap **"Get Started"** to begin the configuration.
2.  **Select Tracking Focus**: Choose between **"Citizenship & PR"** (recommended for most) or **"Permanent Residency"** only. 
3.  **Enter Profile Name**: Provide your name or a nickname for the primary profile.
4.  **Set Critical Dates**:
    *   **PR Landing Date**: Enter the date you became a Permanent Resident.
    *   **Pre-PR Arrival Date (Optional)**: If you lived in Canada as a student or worker *before* becoming a PR, enter your first arrival date. This grants you "half-day" credits toward citizenship.
5.  **Enable Permissions**: If prompted, allow **Location** (set to "Always" for Auto-Tracking) and **Notifications** to receive PR card expiry warnings.

## 2. Dashboard Overview

The Dashboard is your command center.

*   **Header Section**: Shows your name and the current active profile.
*   **Ongoing Trip Button**: Large button at the top to quickly record when you leave or return to Canada.
*   **Status Cards**: 
    *   **Citizenship Card**: Shows your physical presence count (out of 1,095 days).
    *   **PR Status Card**: Shows your residency obligation (out of 730 days in a rolling 5-year window).
*   **Recent Trips**: A quick list of your last three travel entries.
*   **Quick Actions**: Access the **Safe Travel Planner** (Pro) or **Work Hours Tracker** (if applicable).

## 3. Managing Your Trips

### A. Adding a Past Trip Manually
1.  Navigate to the **Trips** tab (airplane icon).
2.  Tap the **"+" (Add)** button in the top right.
3.  Enter the **Start Date** (day you left Canada) and **End Date** (day you returned).
4.  Enter the **Destination**.
5.  Select **Trip Purpose** (Vacation, Business, etc.).
6.  (Optional) Toggle **Special Conditions** if you were accompanying a Canadian spouse or working for a Canadian business abroad.
7.  Tap **"Save Trip"**.

### B. Recording an Ongoing Trip (Real-time)
1.  On the **Dashboard**, tap **"Start New Trip"** as you are leaving Canada.
2.  Confirm the departure details. The app will now show a **"Trip in Progress"** status.
3.  When you return to Canada, open the app and tap **"End Current Trip"**. 
4.  The app will automatically calculate the total days away and add it to your history.

### C. Editing or Deleting a Trip
1.  Go to the **Trips** tab.
2.  Tap on any existing trip to open the editor.
3.  Modify the dates or details and tap **"Update"**, or tap the **Trash Icon** to remove it.

## 4. Tracking Eligibility

Navigate to the **Eligibility** tab for a deep dive into your numbers.

### Citizenship Details
*   **Physical Presence**: Verify your "Days as PR" and your "Effective Days Before PR."
*   **5-Year Window**: View the specific dates the app is currently looking at for your application.
*   **Projected Date**: The app estimates the exact date you can apply. 
*   **Target Date Calculator**: Use the toggle to see what your status will be on a specific future date.

### PR Residency Obligation
*   **Rolling Window**: View how many days you have spent in Canada within the *current* 5-year window.
*   **Surplus Days**: See how many more days you can spend outside Canada before risking your status.

## 5. Family Management (Pro)

If you have a **Horizon Plus** subscription, you can track your entire family:

1.  Tap the **Profile Icon** in the top right of the Dashboard to enter **Settings**.
2.  Tap **"Manage Profiles"**.
3.  Tap **"Add Family Member"**.
4.  Enter their name, relationship (Spouse, Child, etc.), and their specific **PR Landing Date**.
5.  To switch between family members: Go to **Manage Profiles** and simply tap the person's name. The entire app (Dashboard, Trips, Eligibility) will now show *that* person's data.

## 6. Data Import & Export

Keep your records safe or move them to another device.

### Exporting to CSV (Spreadsheet)
1.  Go to the **Trips** tab.
2.  Tap the **Export (Square with arrow)** button.
3.  Select **"Export as CSV"**.
4.  Choose where to save the file (Files app, Email, etc.). This file is perfect for your official IRCC physical presence calculation.

### Backup to JSON
1.  Go to **Settings > Data Management**.
2.  Tap **"Export JSON Backup"**. This creates a file containing all profiles and trip history.
3.  To restore: Tap **"Import JSON Backup"** and select your previously saved file.

## 7. Pro Features & Automation

### Safe Travel Planner
1.  On the Dashboard, tap **"Safe Travel Planner"**.
2.  Tap **"Add Future Trip"**.
3.  Input the dates of a trip you *plan* to take.
4.  The planner will instantly show you how those future days away will push back your citizenship eligibility date.

### Auto-Tracking
1.  Go to **Settings**.
2.  Toggle **"Enable Auto-Tracking"**.
3.  Ensure your phone's system settings for the app are set to **Location: Always**.
4.  The app will now detect when you cross the border. You will receive a notification to "Confirm Trip" when you return.

## 8. FAQ & Troubleshooting

**Q: Why is my Citizenship count lower than I expected?**
*   Check if you entered your **PR Landing Date** correctly.
*   Ensure your **Pre-PR Arrival Date** is set if you want to count half-day credits.
*   Confirm that your trips are logged correctly; overlapping trips can cause calculation errors.

**Q: How are partial days counted?**
*   Following IRCC rules, the app counts the day you leave Canada AND the day you return as **full days in Canada**.

**Q: I lost my phone, is my data saved?**
*   If you have **iCloud Sync** enabled (iOS Only), your data will restore automatically. Otherwise, you must use a **JSON Backup** produced earlier.

**Q: Why aren't my notifications working?**
*   Check **Settings > Notifications** and ensure "PR Expiry Reminders" is toggled on. Also, check your device's system settings to ensure the app has permission to send alerts.
